AfterCheck - AI Fact Checking Companion

AfterCheck is a browser extension that automatically detects and highlights misinformation in ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity responses. The extension wraps a customized version of the Open Fact Check framework, and provides inline corrections, supporting evidence, and confidence scores for ultimate transparency.

Open Bounty - Pricing Engine for Security Vulnerabilities

Open Bounty provides instant, data-driven price estimates for any security vulnerability. Queries are anonymized, aggregated, and fed into publicly accessible dashboards and APIs, giving security professionals real-time insights into trending vulnerabilities, market valuations, and potential exposure.

Lab – Passkeys, Biometrics, and Threat Detection

This lab demonstrates various authentication methods including passkeys, face and voice recognition with liveness detection, anti-spoofing protections, traditional passwords with MFA, and advanced threat detection. It serves as both a showcase of modern authentication methods and a lab for security testing and research.
