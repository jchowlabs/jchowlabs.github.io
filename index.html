AfterCheck - Misinformation Detection

AfterCheck is a browser extension that detects misinformation in ChatGPT, Claude and Perplexity responses. The extension wraps a customized version of the Open Fact Check framework, and has features that include auto-highlighting inaccurate claims and tooltips that include corrections, supporting evidence and confidence scores for ultimate transparency.

Open Bounty - Pricing Security Vulnerabilities

Open Bounty makes it easy for anyone to get a price estimate for any security vulnerability. Simply enter the details into the prompt and instantly receive a data-backed estimate. All queries are anonymized and aggregated into public dashboards and APIs, giving security teams visibility into trending vulnerabilities and potential exposure in real-time.
