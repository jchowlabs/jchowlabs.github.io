Passkeys: Interactive Demo

An interactive, step-by-step walkthrough of Passkey registration and authentication flows. This lab walks through the registration and authentication process including on device key generation, cryptographic signing of registration and authentication challenges, and server-side verification. Rather than treating passkeys as a black box, the demo shows you the underlying steps and trust assumptions that replace passwords, making it easier to understand why passkeys are phishing-resistant and how they fit into modern identity architectures. Designed for practitioners who want to see—not just read—how passwordless authentication actually works.
