Build your own Identity Provider

A lightweight identity-as-a-service provider built to explore the core components of modern identity systems. The article walks through designing an identity directory, implementing federation using SAML and OpenID Connect, and building authentication flows that support passwords with MFA, passkeys, and biometric authentication with liveness detection. It also covers conditional access and threat detection mechanisms—such as impossible travel and behavioral signals—to illustrate how modern identity platforms enforce policy and reduce risk.
